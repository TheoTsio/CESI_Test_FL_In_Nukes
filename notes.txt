docker network create flower-net
SOS: When you run containers in Docker, they are completely sandboxed from the outside world and from each other. Think of them as separate apartments in a massive building. By creating flower-net, you are essentially building a private hallway between those specific apartments so the tenants can talk to each other without having to go out into the public street.
